# 🛡️ ZenTrader AI: Global Market Intelligence

**ZenTrader AI** is a premium, beginner-friendly financial intelligence platform designed to decode global market signals and simplify stock analysis. Built with a sleek, modern Light Theme, it combines real-time data from Yahoo Finance with AI-driven insights to help you make smarter investment decisions.

## ✨ key Features

- **🌍 Global Pulse**: Real-time tracking of Global Indices, USD/INR, Gold, and Crude Oil.
- **📊 Stock Intelligence**: Interactive technical analysis with RSI, Moving Averages, and Sentiment Scoring.
- **🇮🇳 Indian Market Hubs**: Specialized deep-dives for Nifty 50, Tata Group, Oil & Energy, and IT Giants.
- **💼 Private Portfolio Tracker**: Secure, local-only portfolio management with AI health reports and dividend/growth signaling.
- **💎 AI Bargain Finder**: Scans for high-quality stocks trading near their 52-week lows (Buy the Dip).
- **🎯 Smart Salary Advisor**: Custom SIP recommendations for small monthly budgets (starting at ₹500).
- **🎓 Beginner Hub**: Educational tooltips and simplified definitions for every financial term used in the app.

## 🛡️ Privacy & Security
ZenTrader AI is built with privacy at its core:
- **Zero Cloud Storage**: All portfolio data is stored locally on your machine in `portfolio.json`.
- **Anonymized Export**: Privacy Mode toggle hides sensitive valuation numbers during screen sharing or demos.
- **Secure Architecture**: Financial statements and private logs are automatically excluded from version control via `.gitignore`.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
- **Frontend**: Streamlit (Modern Custom CSS)
- **Data Engine**: `yfinance` & `pandas`
- **Visualization**: Plotly Interactive Charts
- **Analytics**: `TextBlob` (NLP) & `Numpy`
